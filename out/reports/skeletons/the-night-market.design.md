## Structure summary

Setup `n_start -> n_meet -> n_promise` (meet shy pangolin Pip), 7-way hub
(6 stalls + direct-to-finale), every stall gift terminal carrying both
return-to-hub and direct-to-`f_steps` edges. Finale: `f_steps` (3-way) into
three independent 3-ending ceremony chains (9 positive endings) plus 4
warm early-evening setbacks inside spokes. Floor path exactly 9 nodes
(reviewer-verified) carried by Pip's shy-to-brave transformation.

## Review verdict (7 categories): all PASS, OVERALL APPROVE

Key judgments the review recorded:

- The keepers-provide-spares device at `f_steps` is the same approved tier-1
  escape hatch as the garden mystery's willow clue restatement: spoke deeds
  are optional richness (kindness/relationship-building), not item-fetch, so
  the floor path is "thin, not hollow" and the quest premise survives.
- Setback signposting fair on 3 of 4 spokes; the toy-mender one was the
  softest and was firmed up in the polish pass.
- Diversity vs the-lantern-festival: acceptable (topology, helper role,
  named-vendor kindness-trades), with the honest note that ambient lantern
  decor pervades beyond the closing ceremony (intrinsic to a night market).
- SHELF RULE recorded: this consumes the 5-8 band's second and FINAL
  hub-and-spoke slot (garden mystery + night market); future 5-8 briefs
  must choose a different topology.
