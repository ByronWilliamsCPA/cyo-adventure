writ, not vigilante theft, in every track. Informational sort into Planners
(Route-Warden Casse; front-loaded rhythm, decision depths 6-17), Climbers
(Rigger Toma; late-clustered, depths 9-25 with a 29-node dawn strand
verified as escalation, not padding), Talkers (Advocate Rhee; steady
every-3-nodes cadence, most decisions per path). Five dignified setbacks
across four rules, each rule STATED before its corner-cut choice, each
setback absorbed by an adult taking the blame while the archive still comes
home by law.
