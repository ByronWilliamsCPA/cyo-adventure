## Structure summary

Moonlit seaside funfair after closing. Setup issues the finale-critical
object (Gran's key ring with the rose charm) at `n_start`; 7-way hub to six
spokes (ticket booth, hall of mirrors, workshop, under-boardwalk, caravan,
organ loft), each clue independently pointing at the lead horse Ondine
(convergent-clue open_map: each sufficient, none necessary); one deliberate
cross-spoke edge (boardwalk -> caravan). Finale: seam on Ondine's saddle
rose -> Gran's hidden repair drawings -> community restoration. 13 endings
(5 warm setbacks, 2 discoveries, 6 positive). Floor path 11 nodes (floor 9),
earned with zero spokes visited.

## Review verdict (7 categories)

1. Arc quality: PASS (floor path earned; spokes escalate by facet)
2. Choice meaningfulness: PASS (setbacks fair-signposted)
3. Open-map structure + cross-spoke edge: FAIL initially: the caravan
   interior was first-encounter-specific while being reachable both from
   the hub and via the cross edge, exposing a named character's one-time
   self-introduction and confession to contradicting replays at tier 1.
   FIXED in repair cycle 1.
4. Reconvergence readability: PASS (finale recap exemplary: cites only the
   path-universal set: key ring, notice text, on-site seam)
5. Band policy fit: PASS (all six scares resolve mundane/human; caretaker
   lands sympathetic; honest flags none/moderate/mild)
6. Fillability: PASS (workshop no-ending spoke justified as the most
   Gran-intimate clue run)
7. Diversity: PASS; shelf guidance recorded: open_map now covered at 5-8
   and 8-11; hold off a third open_map at 8-11 near-term

Initial overall: REVISE (single-spoke fix). After repair: supervisor-verified.
