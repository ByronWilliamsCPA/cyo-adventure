## Structure summary

Setup `n_start -> n_clue` then hub `n_hub` (6-way): five spokes (lettuce
beds, compost corner, tool shed, sunflower row, pond edge), each terminal
offering return-to-hub AND a direct edge to the bottleneck entry
`wc_approach`, which restates the path-universal clues on-site so the reveal
works from any subset of spokes (including zero). Bottleneck chain to a
compassionate reveal (hungry rabbit family) and 4 kind resolutions; 3 gentle
setbacks. Shortest satisfying path 9 nodes (floor 7).

2. Choice meaningfulness: PASS (6-way hub at the band's navigability ceiling
   but concrete and spatial)

Initial overall: REVISE (mechanical). After repair: all fixes
supervisor-verified (zero tunnel mentions in recap nodes, violence "none",
