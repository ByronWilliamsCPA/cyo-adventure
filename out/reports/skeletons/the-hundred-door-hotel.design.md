## Structure summary

Grand hotel, family of five friendly ghosts. Setup plants the whole premise
universally at `n_pearl`; 6-way hub; five spokes (Pearl/lobby, Chef Otto/
kitchens, Wren/winter garden, the twins/guest floors, plus a deliberately
ghost-free observatory spoke that seeds the summons device and the roof-rest
ritual). All success/completion endings confined to the finale cluster (so
PL-20 is structurally unbreakable); a hotel-wide summons makes the reunion
reachable after any subset of spokes; three-way letting-go resolution.
Floor path 19 nodes (floor 14), judged genuinely earned, "thin, not hollow".

## Review verdict (7 categories): all PASS, OVERALL APPROVE

Highlights: re-entry safety verified across all multi-doorway nodes (the
carousel lesson applied by design); names-only-from-ghosts rule held at the
finale; every spook resolves benign on the spot; the letting-go register
judged Coco/Toy-Story-3 warm, at the mature end of 8-11 but in band; flags
honest (violence none, scariness moderate, peril mild).
